Home routine

I wake up at 5 AM, make some coffee, and complete my morning preview, a journal entry. By 5:30, I'm reading, and I won't stop until 7 or so. I start by reading my philosophy, of late the Discourses of Epictetus, before moving into other books. At 7 AM, I start my work, whatever tasks are to be done, and I'll go until noon with a break somewhere in-between.

Noon to one is for lunch and break, and after one, I try to take a nap if my work's on track, usually 90 minutes or so. The afternoon and evening are spent working on tasks and finishing up whatever's left. At 6 PM, I call it a day and start my evening routines: shutdown the computer, turn down the lights, complete my daily review, write in my journal, and read until 8, sometimes 9, when it's time for bed.

Developing

I'm always learning to improve my craft.

I also have an idea for a weekly newsletter or digest: a feature of the things I've done, found, enjoyed, and so on.
